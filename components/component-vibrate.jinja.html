Vibrate API is supported only by Android devices. You can add these to any type of buttons and links and they will work on both iOS and Android, but on Android they will also virbrate when tapped.

iOS doesn't support Virbate API. You can still use the buttons just as regular buttons, but they will not trigger a Vibration when tapped.
